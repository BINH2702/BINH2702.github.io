About me

I am a machine learning researcher and an incoming PhD student in Systems Engineering and Engineering Management at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. I am currently a Research Assistant at VinUniversity.

My research focuses on continual learning and efficient model adaptation. I am particularly interested in how learning systems can acquire new knowledge while retaining previously learned capabilities. My earlier work studied personalized federated learning and federated domain generalization.

Contact: trongbinh2702@gmail.com

Research interests

  • Efficient model adaptation and low-rank adaptation
  • Recursive and latent reasoning models
